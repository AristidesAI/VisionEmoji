# Privacy Policy for VisionEmoji

## Data Collection & Usage
 * This application uses your device's camera exclusively to perform on-device
 * computer vision analysis using a YOLO (You Only Look Once) AI model.

## Privacy Guarantees
 * Camera data is processed entirely on your device
 * No images, video frames, or camera data are transmitted to external servers
 * No data is stored, logged, or retained after processing
 * No personal information is collected or shared
  
## Permissions
 * Camera access is required to capture video for real-time AI analysis.
 * Users can revoke camera permissions at any time through iOS Settings.
   
## Third-Party Services
 * No third-party services, analytics, or tracking libraries are used.
 * All processing occurs locally on the user's device.
   
## Updates
 * This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified
 * of any material changes through app updates.
